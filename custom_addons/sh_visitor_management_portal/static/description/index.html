Visitor Management System-Portal

The "Visitor Management-Portal" gives an efficient way to manage visitors at the portal. You can keep track of who meets whom. You can know visitor's details like visitor's contact details, check-in/out detail, visit the destination, visit type, visit a category, reference name, location, employee name & department name. You can see the visitor's details in the tree view & form view. You can print the visitor's pass with full information.
